About Me

I’m an egoless, self-starting, team playing product designer. When I am not doing product design work, you will find me eating Trader Joe's cookie butter, hiking, camping, traveling, or growing a mustache.

I’m a firm believer that design should be done in the open allowing for early cross-collaboration. I also value user empathy, humility, learning from everyone, and designing with intentionality.

I am currently a senior at BYU-Idaho (Online) pursuing a BS in Web Design & Development and living in Salt Lake City. During my time in school, I’ve had the opportunity to test and refine my design process through real-world UX design experience. Previously, I was a UX design intern @BYU-Idaho, Workfront, and Boncom.

I am looking for future opportunities to grow.
