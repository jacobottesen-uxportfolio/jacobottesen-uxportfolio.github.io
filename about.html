About Me

I am currently a senior at BYU-Idaho (Online) pursuing a BS in Web Design & Development and living in Salt Lake City. When I am not doing UX work, you will find me eating Trader Joe's cookie butter, hiking, doing photography, camping, or growing a mustache.

I started college as a pre-physical therapy major due to multiple career assessments I took. After joining the entrepreneurship society on campus, I learned a new term called “UX design.” I researched the crap out of it and fell in love with the iterative process and methodologies of UX. Soon after that, I changed my degree to web design & development.

Since then it has been a roller coaster of reading blogs, personal projects, internships, and design conferences.

I am looking for future opportunities to grow.
